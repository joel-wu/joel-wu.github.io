Yuheng Wu

I'm Yuheng Wu, an M.S. student in Electrical Engineering at Stanford University, advised by Thierry Tambe. I'm also fortunate to collaborate with Azalia Mirhoseini, Alex Aiken, and Sanmi Koyejo. Before joining Stanford, I received my B.E. in Electronic Information Engineering from Wuhan University.

My research centers on Large Language Models (LLMs), with a focus on reasoning, test-time methods, verification, formal methods, and hardware-software co-design.
